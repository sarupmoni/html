Ninja ZX-10R

The Kawasaki Ninja ZX-10R is a single-seater sports motorcycle with a 998 cc engine. It has a top speed of 299 km/h and a fuel capacity of 17 liters. First released in 2004 and has been updated and revised throughout the years. It combines an ultra-narrow chassis, low weight, and radial brakes. In 2004 and 2005 the ZX-10R won Best Superbike from Cycle World magazine,[2][3] and the international Masterbike competition.
